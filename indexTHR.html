Donate to THE HUMAN ROOT Today!

By: Zaqanah Stephens

This is a campaign supporting THE HUMAN ROOT (https://www.thehumanroot.com) as they raise money for #GivingTuesday. With the goal amount of 10,000 dollars THE HUMAN ROOT will have the opportunity to launch two initiatives. Providing resources to Coney Island for those who are still suffering the effects from Hurricane Sandy and hosting space for Black Indeginous practices and Doula services in predominantly black and brown communities.
